Since 2007

At Mirae English, we have been teaching elementary and middle school students for over ten years, from beginners learning their ABCs for the first time, to high-level students preparing for school exams. Learning English is not easy, but our students put in a lot of effort while having fun at the same time, and are rewarded with a knowledge of the language that will last them a lifetime. Joining Mirae English really is a great way to prepare for your future!

Hours

We have classes every weekday except national holidays, including during school vacation. Beginner classes start at 3:30pm and our latest class finishes at 9:30pm. All of our classes are for one hour.
