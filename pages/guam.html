The Hotel

We spent five nights at the Hilton Guam Resort and Spa at the southern end of Tumon Bay. From our room, we could look out over the entire beach as it curves round to the north.

The Pool

27 degrees every day and with four pools to choose from, it was a relaxing time. The kids loved the waterslide and swimming by themselves in their puddle jumpers.

Out and About

There were many things to do away from the hotel, from the traditional food market and war museums to driving around the island and stopping off to admire the views.
